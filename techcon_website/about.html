Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon began as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the past decade, it has grown into one of the most anticipated annual technology conferences worldwide, drawing thousands of participants from startups, tech giants, and academia. Each year, TechCon explores the newest advancements and fosters collaboration across the global tech community.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to empower innovation, promote diversity in technology, and connect thought leaders with emerging talent. We believe that by fostering an inclusive space for knowledge-sharing and idea generation, we can contribute to building a more ethical and impactful tech future.
